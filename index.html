~/about

A decade of keeping production calm

I've spent more than ten years building and running cloud platforms across cybersecurity, fintech, IoT, fraud prevention, healthcare, web services and smart-home technology. That range means I've seen how the same DevOps problem shows up differently in a regulated bank and a fast-moving startup — and how to solve it for each.

I've worked hands-on in PCI-DSS and HIPAA environments and carried teams through SOC 2 Type II audits — so compliance is something I design into the platform from the start, not bolt on the week before the assessor shows up.

I speak in trade-offs, not buzzwords. Whether you're migrating to the cloud, taming a Kubernetes estate, or trying to make your cloud bill predictable, my job is to make your technology investments deliver measurable business value — and to leave your team stronger than I found it.
